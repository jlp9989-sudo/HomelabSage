Welcome to HomelabSage

A short setup to get you to your first scan in under a minute.

What HomelabSage does

It reads what's running in your homelab (Docker, Home Assistant), checks each piece against its upstream release feed, and uses an LLM to tell you whether an update is critical / high / medium / info for your setup — not "an update exists", but "this one breaks a thing you have, and here's what to do".

You can write notes (e.g. "Plex 1.42 broke my hardware transcode, versionlocked") and the LLM will weigh them when it grades the next Plex update.
